Why this project matters

Getting a job is not only about having a good resume. It is also about answering well in the interview.

Many students struggle in interviews because they:

  • Do not know what kind of questions will be asked.
  • Find it hard to answer tricky and in-depth questions.
  • Feel nervous during interviews.
  • Do not get proper feedback after practice.

This project was built to solve that problem. It gives students a smarter way to practice before attending real interviews.

Main Goal & Benefits

Goal: To help students become more confident, more prepared, and more successful in real interviews.

This is not just a question-answer tool. It is a practice platform that helps you:

  • Prepare for real interviews
  • Improve confidence
  • Handle difficult questions
  • Understand your weak points
  • Perform better in placements

Perfect for: Students, Freshers, Internship seekers, and Job seekers.

What this AI Interviewer does

A complete suite of tools to simulate a real-world technical interview environment.

Analyzes Resume

Reads your uploaded resume and understands your exact skills, projects, and background.

Generates Questions

Creates role-based, unique interview questions directly related to your tech stack.

Realistic Mock Interview

Conducts a back-and-forth verbal interview using advanced voice AI.

Visibility Monitoring

Detects if your face is not visible and gives proctoring warnings for turning left or right.

Evaluates Performance

Shows a final score and detailed feedback at the end to highlight areas for improvement.

How it works

Follow these simple steps to start your practice session.

1

Upload Your Resume

The system reads your resume and understands your profile securely.

2

Start the Interview

The AI asks questions based on your skills, projects, and experience.

3

Answer in Real Time

Students answer using their microphone as if they are attending a real interview.

4

Face Visibility Monitoring

The system checks interview presence and gives warnings when needed.

5

Get Final Feedback

At the end of the interview, students receive a score and performance feedback to improve.

Have doubts about turning on your camera or screen share? Don't worry! The camera, screen, and data only deal with your computer locally. Once the interview ends, the system will automatically turn off your camera, microphone, and stop screen sharing so you can fully relax.
